Skillset

I believe in the T-model. That is, having one strong skill and other smaller skills. This helps you be more well-rounded and diversed, but also makes it easier to communicate with other members of a project who have a different role.

C++

I have been using C++ for many years now, and I believe it helped me understand code more than ANY other language. Having full control over memory is a blessing and a curse.

GitHub / Version Control

I use GItHub or other Version Control (Perforce/SVN) for ALL of my work. I firmly believe in it and use it daily! The power of versioning is unmatched.

Terminal / Bash

Unlike a lot of developers, I am not afraid of the terminal! I love how powerful and fast everything can be when you get rid of a clunky UI. It's my home away from home.

Operating Systems

I have a strong background in many OSes including Windows, Windows Server, Linux (*buntu, CentOS, Arch), MacOSX, and more! Both as desktops and with servers.

Databases

From online game and forum development, I have experience working with DBs in different environments. I also have lot of practice optimizing them with config, table design, and SQL commands/procedures.

Student

I consider myself a student for life. I love to learn, and I always want to do things the 'proper' way, rather than a messy way. Spaghetti belongs in the kitchen, not in my code!
